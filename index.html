About Piratos

Piratos is a hybrid game that combines the Free-to-Play and Play-to-Earn models. You can receive free heroes to play casually. Better yet, acquire NFTs to start earning and maximize your profits.

You can play by yourself or with friends. The strongest bosses are easier to defeat together.

Game mechanics
Move to earn
You can get chests for steps. In the chests you can find items of equipment and coins. Rewards are updated every day.
Collect to earn
The map is full of resources. You can collect them, sell to other players or use to create new items.
Craft
To create valuable resources, you will need special tools
Hunt
Some treasures are guarded by monsters. The higher the level of the monster, the more valuable reward you can get.
Ship
Treasures of raid bosses are heavily guarded. To get to them, the player needs to build a ship and assemble the team.
The ship in the game opens up the following features:
joining in clans
additional quests
new types of chests
more reward
New raid-bosses
Hidden chest
One of the most valuable treasures in the game. Hidden chest can appear at any level for any player. Location — the whole world
Turn on your camera and find your treasure!
You can find up to $500,000
Treasure Island
Treasure Island appears once a month for 3 hours.
Users can get tokens for free every minute if they come near it and point the phone on Island.
The number of tokens received depends on the level of the player.
Scholarship program with character rentals
You can rent characters to other players or rent them for yourself from other players.
